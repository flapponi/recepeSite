Recipe

Lentil curry Noelia style

This is Noelia's version of a lentil curry, a delicious vegetaian meal inspired bythe flavour of Sri Lanka.

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 30 + 20 min 60 min

Ingredients

Lentils: 500g

Rice: 500g

Coconut oil: 2 tbs

Coconut milk: 1 can

1 big onion

3-4 garlic heads

1-2 green chillies

1/2 red pepper

Fresh ginger

Fresh cilandro

Spice mix

Cumin seed: 1 ts

Koriandel seeds: 1 ts

Mustard seeds: 1 ts

Fennikel seeds: 1 ts

Curcuma powder: 1 ts

Hot Madras curry powder: 2 ts

Kanel powder: 1/2 ts

Paprika powder: 1 ts

Salt: 1 ts

Cloves: 4-5

Fresh curry leaves: handfull

Instructions

1

Cut the veggies in small pieces

2

Steer fry the veggies in coconut oil for some minutes

3

Add the spice mix and continue stir fry in low/medium heat for 4-5 minutes

4

In the meanwhile, rinse the lentils until water is translucid

5

Add the lentils and cover with warm water (1-2 cm)

6

Cook for 15-20 minutes with medium heat; add more water if necessary

7

Add coconut milk and cook for additional 5 minutes

8

Let the curry set for 20 minutes before serving

9

Add fresh cilandro as gurnishing

9

Serve with rice or Naan bread
